About us

MIST Thesis Archieve is a platform that holds all the theses and research items done by MIST students and teachers and provides free access to these theses and research information without any barrier to scientific community.Our goal is to help the students and the autority by providing them a better thesis management system where they can find any information about thesis without any hassle and in an organized way.This system also acts as a communication media between ongoing thesis students and their supervisors about their thesis. So it can be said we are here with a complete system to satisfy your needs about thesis !
